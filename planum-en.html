// Three functions
One implement. Three tasks.

PLANUM solves three tasks in a single pass:

  • Levelling — perfect surface flatness for uniform seed placement
  • Loosening — crumb structure in the seed zone, optimal conditions for germination
  • Weed combing — complete removal through 5-row coverage and spring rake
// Design & Components
PLANUM in detail
// Share rows
5 share rows — combination for maximum effect

Five rows of shares in a combination of parallelogram-guided and rigidly mounted elements. The parallelogram shares ensure precise depth control and contour adaptation, the rigid elements provide stability and robustness. Together: complete coverage of all working zones without gaps — significantly better than standard models with 3–4 rows.

// Coverage
100% coverage — no gaps

Five share rows ensure seamless coverage of the entire working area. No untreated strips, no missed weeds. Compared to standard models with 3–4 rows: significantly higher cultivation quality across the entire width.

// Spring rake
Rear spring rake — fine levelling & combing

Spring-tine trailing rake — the decisive difference. Three functions in one component: fine levelling of the surface for perfect flatness, additional loosening of the upper soil layer and complete combing out of weed residues. Uniform seed placement depth across the entire field — prerequisite for homogeneous emergence and maximum yield.
