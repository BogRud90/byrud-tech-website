// How it works
How EXACT works
01
Detect rows

The CMOS camera captures the plant rows in real-time and identifies their position in the image.

02
Recognize plants

The system identifies crop plants based on pigment concentration and distinguishes them from weeds and soil.

03
Target-actual comparison

The detected row positions are compared with the preset inter-row grid — deviations are calculated immediately.

04
Automatic correction

The hydraulic lateral shift block corrects the cultivator position. The working tools automatically align precisely in the center of the inter-row.
