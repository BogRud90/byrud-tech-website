// How it works
The Principle
01
Choose the base

The ROVER cultivator is the base of the system. Choose the right model for your row spacings and crops: ROVER-U, S, R, P or K. Working width 3–12 m.

02
Add modules

Complement ROVER with the modules your operation needs: EXACT for precision guidance, LIXA for liquid fertilization, GRANO for granular application, VELES for seeding. Or TOR, STERN, TURBO for soil cultivation.

03
1 pass = 4 operations

Hoeing, fertilizing, spraying, seeding — all in one pass. Fewer trips, less diesel, less soil compaction. More yield, more profit.
