// Year-round use
365 days a year ready for use

The STERN rotary star harrow is designed for year-round operation:

  • Soil preparation before sowing
  • Loosening and reconsolidation after sowing
  • Prevention of delayed grain germination
  • Improvement of porosity and soil structure
// Star wheels
Star wheels — 50 kg spring pressure, 1 bolt per row

Cast star wheels with 50 kg spring pressure per row — uniform cultivation across the entire width. Adjustment with just one bolt per row — fast and easy adaptation to different working depths. Robust construction, low maintenance.

// Fertilizer & seed option
Simultaneous harrowing, fertilizing and sowing

Optional fertilizer and seed hopper for simultaneous application — harrowing, fertilizing and sowing in one pass. Ideal for direct sowing and cover crop cultivation.

  • Fertilizer and seed hopper combinable
  • Ideal for cover crop sowing
  • Fewer passes, more efficiency
