// Accord metering unit
Mechanical metering system with ground-wheel drive

Application rate continuously adjustable from 5 to 270 kg/ha. The mechanical Accord drive via the ground wheel ensures uniform metering regardless of travel speed.

  • 5 to 270 kg/ha continuously adjustable
  • No electronics — robust and low-maintenance
  • Simple field calibration
// Application methods
Surface, in-row or below the cultivator tines

Flexible depending on crop and requirements — application on the surface, in the row or below the cultivator tines. Simultaneously with mechanical tillage.

  • Surface application — uniform distribution
  • In-row application — directly to the plant
  • Sub-tine application — deep into the soil
