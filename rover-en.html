// Models & Working groups
5 Models.
5 Working groups.

Each ROVER model is optimized for a specific soil type and crop. All groups are interchangeable and can be swapped in under 30 minutes.

ROVER-U
Standard cultivation
Two spring tines (vibro teeth) with flat shares. Designed for standard cultivation on light soils. Effective weed control with minimal soil disturbance.
Light soils
ROVER-S
Enhanced cultivation
Three spring tines (vibro teeth) with arrow shares 150 mm. For medium soils — enhanced cultivation with higher penetration force. Uniform loosening across the entire working width.
Medium soils
ROVER-R
Intensive loosening & ridging
Module of 8 loosening stars with angle adjustment in two planes — for ridging or loosening. With arrow share 150 mm. Optional: mounting of two S-tines (32×12) with arrow shares 150 mm on each parallelogram.
Ridging
ROVER-P
Maximum loosening
Five spring vibration shanks with arrow shares 150 mm. For heavy soils — maximum loosening depth and penetration. Intensive cultivation even on compacted soil layers.
Heavy soils
ROVER-K
Combined cultivation
Two S-shaped spring vibration shanks and three spring tines (vibro teeth) with arrow shares 150 mm. Combined cultivation — loosening and fine tillage in one pass.
Combined
Available for all models: goosefoot shares 200/250 mm and arrow shares 150 mm (standard).
// Components & Options
Attachments &
Protection systems

Protection discs, parallelograms, ridge harrows and deflector shields adapt the ROVER precisely to the crop and soil type. All parts can be retrofitted.

// Steering system
Hydraulic lateral adjustment

The hydraulic steering system shifts all working groups synchronously across the direction of travel — manually or automatically via EXACT. Lateral control range ±25 cm.

  • Control range ±25 cm
  • Manual or fully automatic
  • Response time under 10 ms (with EXACT)
// Parallelograms
Individual ground tracking for each row

Each working group is mounted on a spring-loaded parallelogram that follows the ground profile — independently of adjacent rows. No diving on ground undulations.

  • Spring-loaded 4-point parallelogram
  • Compensation of ±6 cm ground unevenness
  • Individually adjustable spring pressure
// Protection discs
Crop protection directly at the row

Conical protection discs on the hoe tool protect the young plant from soil throw and mechanical damage. Adjustable distance and angle depending on crop stage.

  • Distance 5–30 mm to the plant row
  • For crops from BBCH 11
  • Conical hardened steel discs
// Ridge harrow
Weed control in the ridge zone

Flexible finger weeders work the direct ridge zone between the rows — catching even tiny weed seedlings at the thread root stage that hoe shares cannot reach.

  • Wire tines ∅ 4 mm, spring steel
  • Working speed up to 12 km/h
  • For potato, beet, maize
// Deflector shields
Soil protection for sensitive crops

Foldable deflector shields next to the hoe tool prevent soil throw onto the plant zone. Easy adjustment without tools — reachable from the tractor seat.

  • Foldable, tool-free adjustment
  • For lettuce, spinach, carrots
  • Stainless steel design
// Extensions
Compatible
Attachment systems

EXACT, LIXA, LIXA-O and GRANO can be mounted individually or in combination on any ROVER — for up to 4 operations in one pass.

// Option EXACT
Accuracy up to 1 cm — automatic

Camera-based row guidance

The optical guidance system EXACT detects the rows in real time and corrects the lateral axis of the working groups without GPS. Response time under 10 ms, operation up to 18 km/h.

  • No GPS required
  • 24/7 operation, day and night
  • Response time under 10 ms

Apply liquid components simultaneously

Liquid fertilization & crop protection

LIXA applies UAN, NFS or crop protection agents with row precision — simultaneously with hoeing. Up to 3 liquid components, tank 600–3000 l, CAN bus control.

  • Up to 3 components simultaneously
  • Tank 600–3000 l
  • Herbicide savings up to −40%

Foliar treatment above the plant row

Liquid application above the row

LIXA-O extends the LIXA system with nozzles above the row — for foliar crop protection agents, micronutrients and growth regulators applied directly onto the plant.

  • Over-row nozzles, combinable with LIXA
  • For leafy crops and vegetables
