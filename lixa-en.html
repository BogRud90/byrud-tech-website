// Tank options
Flexible tank sizes

Tanks made of high-quality plastic. Mounted directly on the universal carrier frame — compact, stable, ready for immediate use.

Liquid fertilization directly into the root zone saves up to 40% of plant protection products. The LIXA system delivers UAN, NFS and PPP precisely where they are needed. Control is provided by the Cruise 4 computer with CAN bus connection. Up to 3 liquid components can be applied simultaneously.

// Nozzle options
Flexible for every application type

Various nozzle types for foliar, inter-row and in-row application. Easy change without tools — conversion in just a few minutes directly in the field.

  • Foliar application — full coverage
  • Inter-row application — targeted, spray-gentle
  • In-row application — directly into the root zone
// Option LIXA-O
Precision individual plant treatment

Pinpoint application — up to 40% savings on plant protection products through targeted individual plant treatment. In combination with the EXACT camera system, LIXA-O detects weeds and treats only where necessary.

  • Savings up to −40% PPP
  • Combination with EXACT camera possible
  • Automatic activation per row
